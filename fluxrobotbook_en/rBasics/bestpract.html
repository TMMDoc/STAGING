Best practices

A good robotic solution mainly is based on the following items

Layout

A good layout is most important for a good workflow. Try not to place the robot right in front of the tooling, it will result in many collisions with the part or with the robot.

off centered tooling vs centered tooling

FluxRobot offers a setting that by default the tooling is off centered:

Gripper design

It is essential that the part could be bend without collisions of the gripper with the machine or the robot. It is advised for parts with bends on different corners to start with a 45 degree gripper which covers the maximum final pssible gripping area.

Tip
 See the gripper design chapter for more details

Reachability

Many layout designs do not suffer because positions are not reachable but because the part collides with the robot itself.

A first basic measure is not to place pickup and bend areas closer than …​ to the robot.
