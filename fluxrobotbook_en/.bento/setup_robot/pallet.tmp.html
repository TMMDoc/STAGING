Calibration of Pallets

The accuracy of the pickup pallet is low since parts get their accuracy from the centering table. FluxRobot supports flat and tilted pickup stacks.

The pickup coordinate system will be gained by a calibration plate on the pallet

The pallets are set to the coordinates in World (Robot coordinates ) [-1200 -1500 Z] and [-0 -1500 Z] The Z-Value is measured with the pointer
