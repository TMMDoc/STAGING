Calibration of Pallets

The accuracy of the pickup pallet is low since parts get their accuracy from the centering table. FluxRobot supports flat and tilted pickup stacks. The pickup coordinate system will be gained by a calibration plate on the pallet

It is important that you understand the coordinate system of the pallet and centering table in reference to the machine. The origin of the pallet 50mm inside the stack corners.

Tip
 To minimize collisions best practise is tilt the stack away from the robot.

The calibration of the pallet is done via the 3 point measuring option of the fanuc robot. Teach according the image below

Steps

  1. Press (MENU] key. The screen menu will be displayed.

  2. Select “6 SETUP”.

  3. Press the FI. (TYPE]. The screen change menu will be displayed.

  4. Select "Frames".

  5. Press F3. [OTHER].

  6. Select "Jog Frame". Jog frame entry screen will be displayed.

  1. Move the cursor to the line of the jog frame number you want to set.

  2. Press F2. "DETAIL”. The jog frame setup screen of the selected frame number will be displayed.

  3. Press F2. "METHOD".

  4. Select "Three Point".

  1. teach the points according the image above

  2. after teaching all points you see the position and the tilting angle in the screen. To syncronize your FluxRobot system you could either use transfer all frames via ftp to your computer and use the frame tool or you enter the values manually into Flux
