The gripper has 4x digital IO used for the part sensor or customized functions

  • Pin 1 → FanucIO 1 : signal for part distance sensor

  • Pin 2-4 → FanucIO 1 : Free inputs They are mapped on the Variables P101 - P103 and in reading mode. Update every 5ms.
