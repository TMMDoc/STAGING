A junction box in this context serves as an intermediate connection terminal between the various sensors and devices connected to and around the robot. A detailed report on the connections and components used in the junction box is provided in this document, along with accurate photos and markings.

The components of the device panel are:

  1. Manifold

  2. Vacuum Generator

  3. 4 pin connector terminals

  4. Digital input terminal

  5. 4 Channel Opto-Isolated Board (Opto coupler)

  6. 0v power terminal

  7. 24v power terminal

  8. 24v DC relay
