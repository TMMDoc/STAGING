0The robot interface consists of:

■ Two 42-pin plugs for the cable connection with the robot.

■ An input/output module with digital inputs and outputs for the control signals.

■ A switch panel.

■ An additional key switch with two positions at the rear of the electrical cabinet.

In addition to the signals for synchronization, the plug also contains connections for the EMERGENCY STOP circuit, the enabling condition for press beam movements and the switchover between automatic mode and teach mode

Operating modes

In compliance with the safety regulations of the relevant national standards and laws as well as the additional safety information in this description, it is also possible to operate the TruBend Series 5000 (B23) and 7000 (B28) as usual via the operating panel with manual loading. We differentiate between the operations "TruBend without robot" and "TruBend with robot".

TruBend without robot

When operating without a robot, the TruBend is loaded manually. For this purpose, the press brake is controlled with the operating panel. This setup status corresponds to the description in the operator’s manual of the press brake.

TruBend with robot

During operation with a robot, the sheet is guided by the robot.

Note

In the "TruBend with robot" operation mode, the operating panel must not be removed!

If you select the operating mode "TruBend with robot", the foot switch is partially inactive, e.g. the "PRESS BEAM DOWN" foot switch has no function. Therefore, in robot mode it is not possible to trigger a movement of the press beam with the foot switch.

Operating-mode key switch As standard the external operating-mode key switch is used to switch between operation with and without robot. When doing so the operating panel remains plugged in. The material number for the operating-mode key switch sign is 0858369.

The operating-mode key switch is located next to the On/Off switch.
