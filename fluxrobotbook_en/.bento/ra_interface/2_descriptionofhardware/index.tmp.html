The robot interface consists of:

■ Either of Two 42-pin plugs for the cable connection with the robot with an input/output module with digital inputs and outputs for the control signals ■ Or the Fanuc EtherCAT option .

■ An additional key switch with two positions at the rear of the electrical cabinet.

■ A device panel for all electrical / fluid IO’s

In addition to the signals for synchronization, the plug also contains connections for the EMERGENCY STOP circuit, the enabling condition for press beam movements and the switchover between automatic mode and teach mode
