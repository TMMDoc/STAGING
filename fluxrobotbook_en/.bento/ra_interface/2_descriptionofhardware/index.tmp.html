The robot interface consists of:

■ Two 42-pin plugs for the cable connection with the robot.

■ An input/output module with digital inputs and outputs for the control signals.

■ A switch panel.

■ An additional key switch with two positions at the rear of the electrical cabinet.

In addition to the signals for synchronization, the plug also contains connections for the EMERGENCY STOP circuit, the enabling condition for press beam movements and the switchover between automatic mode and teach mode (see "Signals of the plugs XS7 and XS8 for the robot", pg. 10‐8).
