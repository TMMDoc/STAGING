There are serious dangers when operating a robot

A robot can be connected to the machine via the interface pro vided for this purpose.

The press beam moves at rapid speed and the robot moves.

  • The user must secure the machine and the robot in such a way as to prevent any persons from entering the danger zone (e.g. using safety fencing). Observe the national safety regulations.

The robot is started up while the operator is located within the danger zone of the robot!

  • Make sure that a parking position has been set up for the robot for stand-alone operation.

It may be that "EMERGENCY STOP" push-button does not work!

  • Before activating the robot, download the safety CPU option.Safety chapter from Trumpf manuals

Also always leave the operating panel plugged in during the robot mode.

The EMERGENCY STOP circuit is active, i.e. the "EMERGENCY STOP" push-button is fully functional. If an operating unit with foot switches is plugged in, then this EMERGENCY STOP is also active at all times, since all "EMERGENCY STOP" buttons are connected in series in a circuit.

With a TruBend press brake the operating unit with foot switches is always plugged in in the same way with or without robot interface.

If a robot is not connected, the EMERGENCY STOP circuit of the robot interface is bridged at plug XS8 with the dummy plug

Operating the machine with robot

The integrator must comply with the provisions of this EC Machinery Directive within the area of application of this directive. The integration requires the following documentation:

− Risk assessment

− EU declaration of conformity

− Operator’s manual

Note

  • The user must observe the requirements for manufacturers or integrators outside the area of application of the EC Machinery Directive.

  • The actuation and the electrical installation of the robot are the responsibility of the operator or the integrator. Wiring of the safety-related signals may only be carried out by qualified personnel.

  • The operator must secure (by means of safety fencing, for example) the machine and the robot in such a way that nobody can be in the danger zone during their operation.

Observe the national safety regulations.
