This Motion cycle comprises

  • pickup from a pallet

  • Part separation

  • Double sheet detection

  • Centering table

  • Bending

The important part of this cycle is that the relative position of the pallet will be used to calibrate the part on the centering table

  1. Pickup from a stack in cartesian coordinates in userframe 2

    • this enables the Robot PLC to control several stacks and stack heights

  2. Centering the part in cartesian coordinates in userframe 3

    • this enables a fine calibration of the part before the positioning motion on Bend_1
