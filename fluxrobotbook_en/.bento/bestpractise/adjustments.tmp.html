A real system has tolerances, especially the positioning of a pallet as well as manufacturing accuracy of a gripper. If you want to improve the accuracy of a position it is recommended to adjust the frame of the pickup before.

  • if it is a one time effect then you could adjust just the robot programm

  • if it is a systematic issue over more than one programm it is recommendet to adjust the position in the offline programming system.

The most common adjustment is the centering table to get the part as accurate as possible into the first bend. If you move the frame on the centering table the part will be picked up on a different position and you could improve your accuracy.

Important
 The vacuum gripper picks up the part from below (Z-), so your adjustments need to be in the opposite direction.
Tip
 Jog in the frame of the centering table to jog in a certain direction.
