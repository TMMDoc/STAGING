The motion sequence starts and ends with the home position All motion steps have a unique name that the customer could modify / teach points

The home position is the 90 0 -20 0 20 0 Position for the start of each cycle

When you load a new component with the "add" menu then you could select the usage of the component in the "advanced" section

The transition swing separates two motion blocks (e.t. Pickup-Bending). it contains

  • a safe travel to the next motion block

  • switching of frames

The overall sequence is broken down in sub sequences:

Motions

It is most safe to do motions in 50mm steps in Joint motions with Joint coordinates (no singularities) but we want to give the operator on the machine the chance to fine adjust pickups etc. Therefore we do the final motion before we close the gripper in a adjusted user / tool coordinate system to adjust.

Refer to frames for more information for frames

The nomenclature on the next pages are:

JJ = Joint motion in Joint coordinates

LJ Linear motion in Joint coordinates

JC = Joint motion in Cartesian coordinates in

LC = Linear motion in Cartesian coordinates

UF = UserFrame
