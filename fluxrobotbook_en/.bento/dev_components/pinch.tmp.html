The TCP-positon of a pinch gripper is the center front face of the moving finger

The TCP-Positon of a pinch gipper could be modified:

  1. Unzip CellItems.wad in C:\Program Files\Metamation\Flux\Bin folder.

  2. Change Z position of GripPt of item.curl in 4501 folder (4501-4508 corresponds to Finger gripper). Default is -18,0,234. If you want offset of 50mm, it should be changed to -18,0,284

  3. Change the offset in mechanism.curl. We have to include Shift for all the geometry and if a shift is already defined, increase the z coordinate by 50mm. Also, we have to change the z position of sockets, Fixed and Moving.

  4. Zip the folders back to cellitems.wad. When opening CellItems.wad in 7-zip, we should find various folders at the root.
