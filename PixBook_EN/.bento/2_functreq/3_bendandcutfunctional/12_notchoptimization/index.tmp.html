It should be possible to replace one or all corner notches with a technologically better matching notch. The following notch types need to be supported:

  • Square The notch is replaced by a square matching a squared punch tool.

  • Circle The notch is replaced by a circle matching a circular punch tool.

  • Triangle (Laser) The notch is replaced by a triangle best fitting laser processing.

  • Triangle (Punch) The notch is replaced by a triangle matching a triangle punch tool.

  • None / Remove The notch is replaced by a closed corner, ie. the notch is deleted.

  • Slit The notch is replaced by a slit. Notch optimization is currently done as part of geometry preparation BEFORE the technology assignment in TruTops Boost. This might change in the future. Architectural and Cloud Requirements
