The kernel should be able to recognize 2D contours against a library of known contour 2D templates and replace the identified contours in the 2D model accordingly. Shape recognition comes in two modes:

  • Exact

This mode replaces only exact matching contours which identifies templates from the library.

  • Scale tolerant

This mode replaces resp. identifies templates from the library, that match the shape, but might have a different scaling factor. Technological Requirements
