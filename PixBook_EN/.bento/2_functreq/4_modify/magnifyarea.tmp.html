Magnify Area

This tool is used to magnify certain areas of the part drawing in printouts. You can define a rectangular area to be magnified. The magnified view can be positioned at a convenient location, and you can move, expand/contract the magnification area or change the scale of magnification.

· To add a magnification area, switch to the Magnify Area mode in the Advanced page of the drawing editor. Click, hold down the mouse button and drag to define a rectangular area to magnify. When you release the mouse button, a magnified view is placed below the area:

· To move the magnified area to a new location: just click on top of the magnified area and drag it to the new location:

· To adjust the area being magnified: click in the magnification source rectangle, and drag it to position it at a new location:

· Any number of such magnification views can be created; these are saved with the drawing and loaded back the next time the part is opened.

· To delete a magnified area, hold down the Ctrl key and click on the magnified view.

· To change the scale of a magnification area, hold down the Shift key, click on the magnified area and drag the mouse up and down to change the scale.

The area is redrawn at the new scale

· To expand/contract the area being magnified, position the mouse near the edges of the magnified view, and click/drag.

· The magnified areas are all printed out when the report is printed.
